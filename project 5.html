Building a Healthy Plate

Why a Balanced Meal Matters

A healthy plate gives our body the energy it needs to study, play, think, and grow. It should include colourful fruits, fresh vegetables, grains, and protein-rich food.

Learn more about healthy eating here: MyPlate Nutrition Guide

Smart Snack Choices

Snacking can be part of a healthy diet if you choose wisely. snack on fruits, vegetables, nuts, or yogurt instead of sugary or processed snacks.

Nutrition Tips for a Healthy Lifestyle

  • Eat a variety of foods from all food groups.
  • Limit added sugars and saturated fats.
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water.
  • Practice portion control to avoid overeating.
  • Include physical activity in your daily routine.
  • Get enough sleep to support overall health and well-being.
  • Make use of the MyPlate Nutrition Guide for meal planning.
