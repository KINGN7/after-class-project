    Science
    A subject that teaches us about nature, experiments, space, animals, inventions, chemicals and physical phenomena.
    Coding
    A subject that teaches us how to create games, apps, websites, and animations and also creates a widely adaptive field of understanding about technology.
    History
    A subject that teaches us about past events, cultures, and societies and how we can plan on shaping the future.
